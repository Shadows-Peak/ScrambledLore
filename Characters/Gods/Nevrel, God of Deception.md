Nevrel has been encoded with the [[Oath#Pact|Pact of Deception]] by [[Legyre, The Storyteller|Legyre]]. He has been around since at least before [[The Ring Wars]].

## Pact of Deception
Nevrel must use his powers of [[Chaos Magic]] only to grant capabilities relating to trickery or stealth. He must also only use his powers for his own use so long as it leads to uncertainty over at least one element in any interaction involving it that cannot be discerned by present parties save him. Disobedience will result in [[Gods#Erosion of Will|Erosion of Will]]; this has already been experienced once by Nevrel, who now truly fears it.
