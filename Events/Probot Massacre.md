(==**232 DB**==)
[[Prodigy Child (Probot)|He Who Watches]], who had finally begun to feel comfort in their exertion of forces upon the world from their disembodied form, "possessed" an automaton of destruction made in [[Arthas]] known as "Probot". It followed that, for the next 3 months, He Who Watches would utilize this device and its tools of mass destruction to lay waste to any evidence of [[Humans|human]] life that he would come across. At the end, this automaton was destroyed.
