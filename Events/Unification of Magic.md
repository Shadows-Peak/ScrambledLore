Performed by [[Aternos]] during the end of the [[Age of Chaos]] to fix the unpredictable nature of [[Chaos Magic]] by forming [[Magic]]. It is in general the most powerful spell that is currently active in the universe, and likely the most powerful to have ever been or to ever be performed. Of course, it required the [[Chaos]] energy of about 20% of [[Dragons|Dragon-kind]] which was achieved through their mass slaughtering by Aternos.
