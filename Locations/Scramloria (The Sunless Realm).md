Scramloria was created by [[Xavier]] alongside the [[Order of the Eclipse]] with the goal to give people an escape from magic during the height of the [[Era of Prosperity]]. Of course, it was Xavier's secret goal to use it as a place to keep the [[Prodigy Child (Probot)|Prodigy Child]] away from the rest of the world, and particularly his fellow [[Bearer of the Egg|Bearers of the Egg]].
