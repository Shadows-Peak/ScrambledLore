A coastal nation in [[Navu#Zalde Strip|Zalde]]. Naze, in [[Dragon Tongue]], means “Heart.” Naze has named themselves such for their fortifications and unwavering will to not back down. As a result, they have opted not to partake in the battles of the expansion of land and who will control it. They, however, were forced into participating in [[The Ring Wars]] due to the constant attacks.
